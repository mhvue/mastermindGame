Mastermind Game

Welcome! This game is to guess the numbers.

  • It is a 4 number combination from 0-7. (ex: 1234,4567,6765 ..etc) *duplicate numbers may exist*
  • You get 10 attempts to guess the 4 number combination
  • Number needs to be in correct placement.
  • If you guess the corret numbers, but wrong placment, you will be informed of so (ex: if numbers should be 1234 but you input 1324, that's incorrect)
  • You can view your past guesses and feedback on your right hand side
